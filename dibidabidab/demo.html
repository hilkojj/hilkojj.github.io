Play the demo level in the browser

The first level is basically a little puzzle; certain enemies can only be killed by certain type of arrows, which in turn can be obtained by killing certain enemies.

You can play it here in the browser.

Controls:

Walk left: A
Walk Right: D
Fall Down: S
Jump: Space
Aim: Mouse
Shoot arrow: Left Mouse Button
Retry: R

Hints to beat the level:

Kill Axolotls to obtain Water Arrows

Using the Water Arrows, kill the Fire enemies to cross the bridges

Once found, pick up the key, and take it back to the chest near the floating door.

Shoot a rainbow and walk across it to the exit door.
